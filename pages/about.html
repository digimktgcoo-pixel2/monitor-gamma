About

Who we are and why we built this

NuclearThreatWatch.org is an independent monitoring platform that visualizes publicly available U.S. radiation data from the Environmental Protection Agency's RadNet network in a clearer, more accessible format than the official government interface.

Our Mission

Government data should be easy to read. EPA RadNet provides critical national radiation monitoring around the clock, but its official dashboard is difficult to use on mobile devices and hard to interpret for most people. We built this platform to make that data accessible to everyone.

What We Are Not

This is not an official government website. We are not affiliated with the EPA, FEMA, NRC, or any government agency. This site is an independent data visualization tool only.

Data Source

All radiation data is sourced directly from the EPA RadNet Central Data Exchange (CDX). We do not modify, model, or forecast radiation levels. We display the raw EPA readings.

Technology

This platform is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA) — a static site with no backend servers. It works offline after first load and can be installed on any device like a native app. All data fetching happens directly between your browser and EPA servers.
