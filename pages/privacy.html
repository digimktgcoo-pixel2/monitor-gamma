Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — Section 13.0

Last updated: 2025. This is a preliminary policy. Consult an attorney before commercial deployment.

Data We Collect

This site is a static Progressive Web App. We do not operate backend servers or databases. We do not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information.

Analytics

We may use anonymized analytics tools (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics or Google Analytics in anonymized mode) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. If analytics are enabled, no personally identifiable information is collected. Analytics data is used only for platform improvement.

Cookies

This site does not set tracking cookies. Browser caching and service worker caching are used to enable offline functionality — this data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

This site fetches data from EPA RadNet servers. Your browser may transmit your IP address to EPA servers as part of normal HTTP requests. We do not control EPA's data practices. See EPA's Privacy Policy.

Map tiles are served by CartoDB. See CartoDB's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

Ad placeholders exist in the site code but are disabled in the current version. When advertising is enabled, an advertising disclosure will be added to this policy.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy questions, use our Contact page.
